Tips for Great Events
  • Be authentic — share your genuine cultural experience and personal connection.
  • Tell the story behind the tradition — why it matters and what it means to you.
  • Create an interactive experience — let attendees participate and ask questions.
  • Use high-quality photos that showcase the cultural elements.
  • Be welcoming to all levels of cultural knowledge and experience.
